### **Glucagon should be prescribed for all individuals at increased risk of clinically significant hypoglycemia, de-fined as blood glucose <54 mg/dL (3.0 mmol/L), so it is available should it be needed. Caregivers, school personnel, or family members of these individuals should know where it is and when and how to administer it. Glucagon administration is not limited to health care professionals.**
